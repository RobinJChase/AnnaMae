WELCOME TO

ANNA MAE’S

Anna Mae’s is a great family owned & operated establishment and an excellent choice to eat before or after you take in the St. Jacob’s Farmers Market. We are less than 30 minutes from Stratford, Waterloo, Drayton & St Jacob’s. Our quaint village also has a Cheese Outlet, Country Market, Hardware Store, Mennonite Furniture Shop and quilt shops! Come in and try our famous Broasted Chicken and Made in-house Mennonite Meals & Baking!

We bake a variety of 18+ Pies made in-house everyday!

Reservations: We accept reservations for groups of 10 or more, or for our Buggy Booth (seats 4 guests) by calling 519-595-4407.

We also have GLUTEN CONSCIOUS baking! Please see our Bakery section for a list of all of our made in-house products.

*Please note: the following ingredients are used in our facility: WHEAT, EGGS, MILK, SOY, PEANUTS, TREE NUTS, MUSTARD, & FISH. SESAME & SULPHITES can also be found within some ingredients in our facility. Although we try our best, cross contamination is always a possibility.
