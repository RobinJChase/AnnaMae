IT’S MADE IN-HOUSE

Our items are made in-house and baked daily. We use fresh (when available) or frozen raw fruits to make all of our fruit pie fillings. Come in and try the difference that fresh makes! Please call 519-595-4407 to reserve your baking to avoid disappointment!

We decorate cakes too! Chocolate, Carrot, Marble, White Cake, or Black Forest. Available in many sizes.

*Please note we do not add preservatives to our baking*

*Please note: we use the following ingredients in our facility: WHEAT, EGGS, MILK, SOY, PEANUTS, MUSTARD, & FISH. TREE NUTS & SULPHITES can also be found in some ingredients in our facility. Although we try our best, cross contamination is always a possibility.

*We use the word Homemade to refer to our products being made in-house by our wonderful employees!
